視頻資料 / Video Demonstration

影片展示使用2500°C高溫氧氣切割槍對鋼板與本公司專利建材進行切割測試。

鋼板迅速被切穿，而本材料經歷更長時間的高溫切割後，僅表面輕微碳化，未被穿透，顯示出極強的阻燃性能與熱穩定性。

下方照片為切割測試後的樣本實物：左為鋼板（已斷裂），右為纖維膠合建材（表面碳化，內部完整）。

The video demonstrates a 2500°C oxy-fuel torch test comparing standard steel and our proprietary composite panel. While the steel plate is quickly cut through, our material withstands significantly longer exposure with only minor surface charring.

Below are post-test photos: the severed steel on the left, and our fiber-reinforced panel on the right — visibly intact, proving superior fire resistance and thermal durability.
