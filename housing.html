Our DIY Housing Solution for the Poor

Our approach combines modular molds with patented eco-binder technology. A simple and cost-effective system includes a small shredder and mixer, enabling families to directly build houses themselves.

In collaboration with environmental NGOs and sustainability funds, we provide molds and technical guidance through volunteer support. Families can build their homes with dignity.
