Our DIY Housing Solution for the Poor

Our approach combines modular molds with patented eco-binder technology. A simple and cost-effective system includes a small shredder and mixer, enabling poor families to participate directly in home construction.

In collaboration with environmental NGOs and sustainability funds, we provide molds and technical guidance through volunteer support. Families can build high-quality, pollution-free homes by themselves.

With just 3 people and 5–7 days, a durable and dignified house like the one shown here can be completed. This is not just shelter — it’s empowerment through innovation.
