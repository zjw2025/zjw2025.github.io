Our Solution: DIY Molds & Community Housing

This is a simple and effective approach using our patented molds and binder system. The process requires only a small-scale shredder and mixer. In collaboration with environmental NGOs and green foundations, we provide molds and volunteer technical support to families in need.

Low-income families can construct their own homes with just three people in 5–7 days. These houses are high quality, non-toxic, and environmentally friendly — turning waste into dignified shelter.
