🌍 全球固废污染與住房問題

全球固废污染和住房短缺问题日益严重。根据 UNEP 2024 年《全球塑料污染报告》，每年产生约 3.8 亿吨塑料废弃物，回收率不足 15%，大量填埋或焚烧导致土壤、海洋和空气污染（CN109181074A）。

纺织品废弃物年产量超 1.1 亿吨，仅 20% 被回收，只有 1% 被循环制成新衣物（UNEP 2024）。

与此同时，UN-Habitat 2025 年报告显示，约 1.7 亿人无家可归，16 亿人居住条件恶劣，尤其在人口密集的贫困地区，住房需求迫切。

🌍 Global Solid Waste and Housing Crisis

Global solid waste pollution and housing shortages are becoming increasingly severe. According to the UNEP Global Plastic Pollution Report 2024, approximately 380 million tons of plastic waste are generated annually, with a recycling rate of less than 15%. A large portion is either landfilled or incinerated, causing soil, ocean, and air pollution (CN109181074A).

The annual production of textile waste exceeds 110 million tons, with only 20% being recycled and just 1% turned into new clothing (UNEP 2024).

At the same time, the UN-Habitat 2025 report indicates that around 170 million people are homeless and 1.6 billion live in substandard conditions, with urgent housing needs particularly concentrated in densely populated, low-income regions.
