志愿者如何指导建造 3000 美元房屋

How Volunteers Guide $3000 Housing Construction

该技术的推广不靠商业销售，而是由经验丰富的志愿者团队进行“一对一指导”完成。通过线上教学、环境实场协助和标准化工具包，任何有基础动手能力的团队均可以10天内建成一间清淨、环保、居住性良好的房屋。

This low-cost housing technology is deployed not through sales, but through a trained global volunteer network. Volunteers provide one-on-one guidance via online tutorials, in-person support, and standardized toolkits. Any small team with basic hands-on ability can build a clean, eco-friendly, livable house in just 10 days.

如何用5亿美元清除10万吨海洋垃圾

How $500 Million Can Remove 100,000 Tons of Ocean Plastic

本项目计划100个工作日内，使用5亿美元清除太平洋中的10万吨塑料垃圾，预算结构如下：

This plan allocates $500 million to clean up 100,000 tons of ocean plastic waste from the Pacific within 100 working days. Budget breakdown:

  • 平台建造与出海作业：使用总预算 1/6，建造6套模块化清洗平台
  • 燃料和后勤支持：使用 1/3，包括太阳能、电池、垃圾压缩系统
  • 打捕和处理垃圾：使用 1/3，进行分类、分解、回收
  • 志愿者管理和国际协作：使用 1/6，支持远程指挥和信息公开
  • Platform construction and deployment: ~1/6 of total for 6 mobile clean-up units.
  • Fuel and logistics: ~1/3, includes solar power, batteries, compression systems.
  • Collection and processing: ~1/3 for capture, breakdown, and recycling.
  • Volunteer and coordination: ~1/6 for global team management and open transparency tools.

平台完成清除后，将移动到其他海域，形成持续操作机制，办力解决全球性塑料污染问题。

Once the Pacific zone is cleared, platforms can migrate to other regions, building a continuous cleanup system and accelerating global marine restoration.
