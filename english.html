Introduction

An integrated solution for composite solid waste recycling and rapid marine plastic cleanup:

  • Innovation: Polymer solidifies within 6 hours
  • Zero Pollution: No incineration, no secondary emissions
  • Waste-to-Resource: Patented gelling agent made from volcanic ash, flue dust, and blast furnace slag
  • Global Deployment: Scalable, decentralized modular systems
  • Community Empowerment: Enables low-cost housing and job creation in waste-affected regions

This system is built on an innovative, patented composite gelling technology.
