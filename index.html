Working Together for a Sustainable Future

Using waste to solve waste – Patented technology turning marine and textile waste into durable housing materials

Utilizing the simplest and most direct approach, we aim to solve global solid waste problems through non-commercial technology integration.

With $3,000, a high-quality, zero-pollution, B1 fire-rated, 25–30 MPa strength, 30-year lifespan house can be built for someone in need.

With $500 million – one-sixth of which goes toward a relocatable operating platform – we can clean up 100,000 tons of Pacific plastic waste in 100 days, generating 300,000 cubic meters of landfilling material. Afterward, the platform can be moved to clean other marine zones.
