Working Together for a Sustainable Future

This project introduces an innovative solution to turn waste into sustainable building materials — solving both solid waste management and affordable housing for low-income communities. It is designed to be low-cost and highly efficient, with global scalability and zero toxicity.

  • ✅ Converts industrial waste into durable building materials.
  • ✅ Builds a complete house for less than $3000.
  • ✅ Certified B1 fire resistance and 40-year durability.
  • ✅ Plans to remove 100,000 tons of ocean waste in 100 days.
  • ✅ Open-source technology; we do not accept donations.
