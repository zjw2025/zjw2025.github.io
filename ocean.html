特別值得關注的是：在這 10 年內，海洋仍將不斷聚集大量新增塑膠垃圾。而「海洋清理」組織是否有長期規劃以應對新增污染？每年的實際處理量是否足以抵消新增量？這些問題至今尚未有明確說明。

本系統總成本為 5 億美元，其中六分之一投入於改造可移動操作平台。當太平洋漂流帶垃圾清理完畢後，平台可轉移至其他海域繼續作業，實現長期、可延續的清潔行動。

更重要的是，該平台亦可應用本公司專利膠凝技術，利用處理後的固體廢料為太平洋島嶼小國及沿海貧困地區提供可負擔的環保住宅，創造附加社會價值。

我們認為，能夠簡單解決的事情，是否有必要動用大量公共資金（納稅人資金）進行繁瑣而高成本的處理？

本項目已獲得發明專利：「An Efficient and Environmentally Friendly Marine Plastic Waste Treatment System and Its Preparation Method」，申請號：819457，提供可行的技術依據。
ENGLISH:
It is especially worth noting that over the span of ten years, a significant amount of new plastic waste will continue to accumulate in the ocean. Has The Ocean Cleanup proposed a long-term strategy to deal with this continuous influx? What is their projected annual processing capacity, and will it be sufficient to offset the ongoing pollution? These key questions remain unclear.

In our solution, the total cost is $500 million, with one-sixth invested in the construction of a relocatable operation platform. Once the Pacific garbage patch is cleared, the platform can be transferred to other ocean regions to continue the cleanup mission — enabling long-term, sustainable operation.

More importantly, the platform can also apply our patented solidification technology to convert treated marine waste into affordable and eco-friendly housing for Pacific island nations and impoverished coastal communities, adding long-term social value.

We believe that if a problem can be solved in a simple and efficient way, is it truly necessary to spend massive amounts of public (taxpayer) funds on complicated and costly solutions?

This project is supported by a granted invention patent: “An Efficient and Environmentally Friendly Marine Plastic Waste Treatment System and Its Preparation Method” — Application No. 819457 — as a solid foundation for implementation.
